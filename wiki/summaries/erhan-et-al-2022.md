# Erhan et al. 2022

**Summary**: A study on how the digitalization of leadership positively influences the four dimensions of employee innovative work behavior in the Turkish textile industry.

**Sources**: Erhan et al. - 2022.md

---

This paper explores the shift from conventional to digital leadership and its impact on [[innovative-work-behavior]] (IWB). Data collected from 320 department managers in the Turkish textile industry confirms that [[digital-leadership]] significantly and positively affects all four dimensions of IWB:
1. Idea exploration
2. Idea generation
3. Idea championing
4. Idea implementation
(source: Erhan et al. - 2022.md, p. 1535-1536)

The authors define digital leadership based on the Upper Echelons Theory, meaning leaders who possess digital skills, follow technological advancements, and are proactive in their approach create a climate that fosters innovation (source: Erhan et al. - 2022.md, p. 1527, 1537). By establishing a digital workplace and providing necessary digital culture, digital leaders make employees more willing to take calculated risks and find novel solutions.
