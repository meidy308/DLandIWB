# Soediro et al. (2025) – Antecedents of lecturers’ innovative work behavior in a private university: Creative process engagement as a mediator

**Summary**: This study identifies digital leadership, growth mindset, and organizational climate as key drivers of lecturers' innovative work behavior (IWB), with creative process engagement (CPE) serving as a critical mediator that bridges the gap between these antecedents and actual innovation.

**Sources**: [[Soediro et al. - 2025.md]]

---

## Bibliographic Details

- **Author**: Moses Soediro (Ciputra University), Lieli Suharti, Komala Inggarwati, Andrian Dolfriandra Huruta (Satya Wacana Christian University)
- **Year**: 2025
- **Journal**: Knowledge and Performance Management, 9(1), 139-147
- **Methodology**: Quantitative
- **Sample**: 200 lecturers from social sciences and humanities faculties at six major private universities in Surabaya, Indonesia
- **Analysis**: Partial Least Squares Structural Equation Modeling (PLS-SEM) using SmartPLS
- **Data collection**: Structured questionnaires collected over two months

## Hypotheses and Results

| Hypothesis | Description | Result |
|---|---|---|
| H1 | GM → IWB (+) | **Supported** (β = 0.172, p = 0.001) |
| H2 | OC → IWB (+) | **Supported** (β = 0.251, p = 0.000) |
| H3 | DL → IWB (+) | **Supported** (β = 0.300, p = 0.000) |
| H4 | GM → CPE → IWB | **Supported** (31.84% mediation, T = 4.683, p < 0.01) |
| H5 | OC → CPE → IWB | **Supported** (43.53% mediation, T = 2.951, p < 0.01) |
| H6 | DL → CPE → IWB | **Supported** (47.94% mediation, T = 4.910, p < 0.01) |

## Key Findings

### Digital Leadership as the Primary Driver
Digital leadership exhibited the most significant direct impact on innovative work behavior (source: Soediro et al. - 2025.md, p. 144). Leaders who effectively integrate digital tools and promote a culture of adaptability facilitate the utilization of technological resources for innovative educational practices (source: Soediro et al. - 2025.md, p. 144).

### The Mediating Role of Creative Process Engagement
The study highlights CPE as a transformative force, accounting for a substantial portion of the variance in IWB ($R^2 = 76.1\%$). CPE acts as the primary mediator, facilitating the implementation of independent variables into daily work practices through experimentation and innovative problem-solving (source: Soediro et al. - 2025.md, p. 144).
- Total effect of DL on IWB is partially mediated by CPE (47.94%).
- Total effect of OC on IWB is partially mediated by CPE (43.53%).
- Total effect of GM on IWB is partially mediated by CPE (31.84%).

### Growth Mindset and Resilience
Lecturers with a growth mindset are better equipped to adapt to digital transformations and develop novel teaching methodologies (source: Soediro et al. - 2025.md, p. 144). A growth mindset allows individuals to be receptive to challenges and experimentation (source: Soediro et al. - 2025.md, p. 143).

## Practical Implications
- Higher education institutions should focus on developing policies that encourage collaboration, digital competence, and knowledge-sharing.
- University leaders should foster an environment that supports active creative engagement to bridge the gap between digital strategies and actual innovative outcomes.

## Limitation
- The study focuses solely on social sciences and humanities faculties in private universities in Surabaya, which may limit generalizability to other sectors or public institutions.
- The use of self-reported questionnaires may introduce common method bias.
