# Çetinkaya and Sürücü (2025)

**Summary**: A study exploring how digital leadership influences innovative work behavior through the mediating role of digital organizational culture within the IT sector in Istanbul.

**Sources**: Çetinkaya and Sürücü - 2025.md

---

This research involved 404 employees operating in the IT and technology sector in Istanbul, Turkey. It investigated the interconnected relationship between [[digital-leadership]], [[digital-organizational-culture]], and [[innovative-work-behavior]] (source: Çetinkaya and Sürücü - 2025.md, p. 236).

The findings demonstrate that digital leadership has a direct, positive effect on both digital organizational culture and innovative work behavior. Furthermore, digital organizational culture significantly mediates the relationship between leadership and innovation. This implies that leaders use their influence to construct a supportive, technologically agile culture, which in turn acts as a catalyst for employees to generate practical innovations (source: Çetinkaya and Sürücü - 2025.md, p. 242-243).
