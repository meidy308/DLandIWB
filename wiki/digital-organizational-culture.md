# Digital Organizational Culture

**Summary**: The shared values, beliefs, and practices that shape how digital technologies are integrated into the workplace.

**Sources**: Çetinkaya and Sürücü - 2025.md

---

Digital organizational culture reflects the extent to which a company embraces digital technology, agility, and innovation. Key characteristics include an openness to change, strong collaboration via digital platforms, an emphasis on continuous learning, and employee empowerment (source: Çetinkaya and Sürücü - 2025.md, p. 238).

It serves as a critical mediating catalyst. Initiated by strong [[digital-leadership]], a positive digital culture helps translate new ideas into tangible outcomes. By valuing experimentation and viewing failure as a learning opportunity, the culture unleashes the creative potential of the workforce, resulting in increased [[innovative-work-behavior]] (source: Çetinkaya and Sürücü - 2025.md, p. 242-243).
