# Communication as Constitutive of Organization

**Summary**: A perspective that views communication as the fundamental process that creates and sustains organizations.

**Sources**: Afif and Fitrianingrum - 2025.md

---

Communication as Constitutive of Organization (CCO) perspective is used to explain the role of [[organization-experience]] in shaping meaning, interaction patterns, and individuals' adaptive capacities when facing changes. This perspective highlights how social and communicative experiences forge the foundation for [[innovative-work-behavior]] (source: Afif and Fitrianingrum - 2025.md, p. 3110).
