# Organization Experience

**Summary**: The accumulated knowledge, skills, and social interactions gained from active participation in organizational activities.

**Sources**: Afif and Fitrianingrum - 2025.md

---

Organization experience encompasses practical application in the work environment (*framing*), involving team members in processes (*co-creating*), and establishing effective communication and relationships (*connecting*) (source: Afif and Fitrianingrum - 2025.md, p. 3114-3115).

It proved to be the most dominant factor influencing [[innovative-work-behavior]] among Gen Z workers in Central Java. Experience helps individuals develop broader, creative problem-solving methods, courage in decision-making, and collaboration skills that traditional training or technology cannot easily replace (source: Afif and Fitrianingrum - 2025.md, p. 3119-3120).
