# Innovation Capabilities

**Summary**: The aptitudes, resources, and methodologies that empower individuals and businesses to nurture novel ideas into practical innovations.

**Sources**: Ahmed et al. - 2024.md

---

Innovation capabilities refer to a company or individual's capacity to conceptualize, develop, and manage new technologies, products, or processes (source: Ahmed et al. - 2024.md, p. 3611). In terms of digital innovation, it also involves "digital capability"—the proficiency to manage digital technology to create these solutions (source: Ahmed et al. - 2024.md, p. 3615-3616).

These capabilities serve as a mediator. Driven by a strong [[learning-orientation]], having concrete innovation capabilities translates directly into [[innovative-work-behavior]] (source: Ahmed et al. - 2024.md, p. 3614-3615).
