# Job Crafting

**Summary**: The self-initiated actions employees take to reshape their job boundaries by aligning their knowledge, skills, and psychological resources with work demands.

**Sources**: Huang et al. - 2026.md

---

Job crafting is a proactive behavior where employees autonomously adjust their work tasks, methods, and interpersonal relationships to better suit their personal needs and goals. Grounded in [[self-determination-theory]], it embodies employee autonomy and serves as a crucial catalyst for innovation (source: Huang et al. - 2026.md, p. 3).

## Dimensions

Standard literature (e.g., Tims et al., 2013) identifies dimensions such as:
- **Increasing structural job resources**: Seeking opportunities for professional development and learning.
- **Increasing social job resources**: Seeking feedback and support from colleagues and supervisors.
- **Increasing challenging job demands**: Seeking more complex tasks or taking on new responsibilities.
- **Decreasing hindering job demands**: Reducing aspects of the job that interfere with performance (source: Huang et al. - 2026.md, p. 5, 10).

## Relationship with Other Variables

### As a Mediator
- **Digital Leadership and IWB**: Job crafting mediates the relationship between [[digital-leadership]] and [[innovative-work-behavior]] (Point estimate = 0.075, 95% CI [0.014, 0.152]) (source: Huang et al. - 2026.md, p. 8).
- **Sequential Mediation Path**: Job crafting initiates a sequential mediation chain: [[digital-leadership]] $\rightarrow$ [[job-crafting]] $\rightarrow$ [[innovation-efficacy]] $\rightarrow$ [[innovative-work-behavior]] (Point estimate = 0.067, 95% CI [0.033, 0.124]) (source: Huang et al. - 2026.md, p. 8).

### As a Consequent
- **Digital Leadership**: Digital leadership promotes job crafting by providing advanced digital tools, infrastructure, and flexible work models that increase autonomy (source: Huang et al. - 2026.md, p. 3).

## Measurement

- **Tims et al. (2013)**: A 15-item scale selected from the original 21-item scale. Items include "I make efforts to enhance my skills" and "I occasionally determine my own approach to work" (source: Huang et al. - 2026.md, p. 5).
