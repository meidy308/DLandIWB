# Problem-Solving Creativity

**Summary**: The ability to generate novel ideas and original methods when addressing complex workplace challenges.

**Sources**: Hastiti et al. - 2025.md

---

Problem-solving creativity provides individuals and teams the space to think outside traditional boundaries, question existing assumptions, and locate new connections between distinct data points. 

[[digital-leadership]] has a strongly significant, positive impact on fostering problem-solving creativity (source: Hastiti et al. - 2025.md, p. 50-51). Digital leaders can facilitate this by introducing collaborative software and data analytics that break down traditional communication barriers. According to [[social-role-theory]], this relationship can be moderated by gender, with digital leadership showing a significant influence on creative problem-solving for women (source: Hastiti et al. - 2025.md, p. 52).

While creativity in problem-solving logically underpins the generation of ideas, research indicates it does *not* necessarily translate directly into [[innovative-work-behavior]] (source: Hastiti et al. - 2025.md, p. 51). The lack of a direct relationship suggests that while creativity can spark ideas, structural, cultural, or resource barriers may prevent these ideas from being practically implemented.
