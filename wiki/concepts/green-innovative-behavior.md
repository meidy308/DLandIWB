# Green Innovative Behavior

**Summary**: Actions targeted at inventing and implementing sustainable solutions—such as new products, processes, or ideas—aimed at achieving environmental goals.

**Sources**: Khan et al. - 2025.md

---

Green innovative behavior reflects the extent to which individuals and teams create or improve products, processes, or ideas aimed at environmental goals (source: Khan et al. - 2025.md, p. 2). It is a vital component of a sustainable business model transition, which entails adopting green technologies and sustainable supply chain management (source: Khan et al. - 2025.md, p. 2).

## Dimensions

While often treated as a unidimensional construct in behavioral studies, it encompasses:
- **Product Innovation**: Development of environmentally friendly products.
- **Process Innovation**: Implementation of sustainable production or operational methods.
- **Idea Generation**: Creating original solutions to environmental problems.

## Relationship with Other Variables

In the research by [[khan-et-al-2025]], the following relationships were established:
- **Digital Leadership**: Acts as a critical predictor, primarily through the facilitation of a green climate (source: Khan et al. - 2025.md, p. 6).
- **Green Organizational Climate**: Serves as a direct predictor and mediator. A positive green climate significantly enhances green innovative behavior (β = 0.59, p < .001) (source: Khan et al. - 2025.md, p. 9).
- **Strategic Agility**: Moderates the indirect path from leadership to green innovation. The behavior is most pronounced in agile firms where leadership can effectively pivot resources toward sustainable goals (source: Khan et al. - 2025.md, p. 11).

## Measurement

- **Scale**: 3-item scale developed by Verma and Singh (2022).
- **Sample items**:
  - "I create new green ideas for environmental improvements."
  - "I often search out new green working methods, techniques, or instruments."
  - "My ideas generate original solutions to environmental problems."
- **Likert**: 5-point scale (1: Strongly Disagree, 5: Strongly Agree).
