# Conservation of Resources Theory

**Summary**: A psychological theory stating that individuals strive to obtain, retain, protect, and foster resources.

**Sources**: Afif and Fitrianingrum - 2025.md

---

The Conservation of Resources (COR) Theory emphasizes the importance of accumulating individual resources to produce positive workplace behaviors. The theory supports the idea that accumulated resources, such as those gained through [[organization-experience]] and strong [[digital-leadership]], encourage [[innovative-work-behavior]] (source: Afif and Fitrianingrum - 2025.md, p. 3109-3110).
