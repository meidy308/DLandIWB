# Learning Orientation

**Summary**: An active dedication to continuously enhancing skills, understanding, capabilities, and expertise.

**Sources**: Ahmed et al. - 2024.md

---

Learning orientation is an internal motivator that drives individuals to actively seek challenges and accumulate valuable expertise over time (source: Ahmed et al. - 2024.md, p. 3612). At the organizational level, it reflects values emphasizing the creation, sharing, and application of essential knowledge (source: Ahmed et al. - 2024.md, p. 3615).

It serves as a critical mediator. Enhanced by [[digital-leadership]] and strong [[leader-member-exchange]], a learning orientation is a direct driver of [[innovation-capabilities]] because employees invest more effort to acquire the foundational understanding needed to create novel solutions (source: Ahmed et al. - 2024.md, p. 3614).
